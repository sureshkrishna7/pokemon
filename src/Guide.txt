Welcome to Pokemon:

There are 2 game modes in this type of game. Safari Zone & Battle:

You can explore the City and find pokemon to battle.
You have the ability to change pokemon, use Items, fight or run.

You can also enter the safari zone to capture new Pokemon.
Here, you cannot battle pokemon, but you can use items to 
assist you in capturing pokemon.
Rocks will make it easier to catch a pokemon, but more likely
to run.
Bait will make the pokemon less likely to run.
Use the safari ball to catch safari pokemon

Explore the map, battle pokemon, and capture pokemon to add
to your team.

Have Fun!
